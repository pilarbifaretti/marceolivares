Cultivating Vibrant Culture: Redefining Rituals

Fostering Connection, Collaboration, and Growth Through Innovative Initiatives

RedBox Retreat

Objective: To create a weekend event with a balanced agenda between work, collaboration, and connection.

Output: An annual event where the entire company travels to cities/towns within 3 hours of Mexico City. Activities include:

  • Discussing the future of the company (numbers, expectations, vision, etc.)
  • Presenting inspirational topics and new models applicable to projects
  • Providing clarity on the agenda of the rituals that will remain in place throughout the year
  • Activities to connect with new members, including round table discussions on topics each person is an expert in or passionate about
  • GoodNightKiss: a gift given at the end of each event as a token of appreciation, with tips on things to do and visit
  • A night of board games and personalized cocktails

Welcome Kit

Objective: To create a kit with an important and consistent ritual that makes new members feel welcome and embodies the company's DNA.

Output: A kit with an onboarding manual containing information such as the company's history, teams and their members, main methodology used, values, internal language, and tips from the team to get started. The kit includes:

  • The book "Look at More" by Andy Stefanovich, signed by all members of the new member's area
  • Bookmarks with the company's annual mindset
  • A chocolate and beer for delight and celebration
  • The keys to their locker and access to the building
  • A personalized pen and notebook for the first days

Additionally, a detailed agenda is created for the first week, with the first meeting being with the team: bread, coffee, and a warm welcome.

Internship Program

Objective: To create a consistent and attractive internship plan.

Output: A 4-month program where a designer spends 2 months in the design area and 2 months in the intelligence area. The program includes a welcome kit, financial incentive, and the opportunity to receive a diploma from RedBox Academy. Over 60% of participants receive full-time job offers from the teams. Additionally, it welcomes exchange students from other countries, with the process managed to validate professional internships from European universities.
