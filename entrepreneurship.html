AXIAL: A Personal Journey in Entrepreneurship

Embracing the Fusion of Minimalism and Functionality in Product Design

Entrepreneurship has been an incredible journey for me. For three years, some close friends and I embarked on the adventure of launching our very own product design brand right here in Mexico. We poured our hearts and souls into this venture, and the results were nothing short of amazing.

From the very beginning, we hit the ground running. We showcased our creations at more than 10 bazaars, where our designs caught the eye of many, including major restaurants across Mexico who wanted our unique touch for their table and event decorations.

And so, AXIAL was born. A brand that reflected our shared vision and passion for minimalism and geometric shapes. We believed in creating design elements that are not only visually stunning but also highly functional, seamlessly blending form and function.
